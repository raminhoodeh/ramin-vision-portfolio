# Product Innovation Process Mindset Overview

**Experience**

Building/managing products requires making decisions on complex considerations, all while... Collaborating with different departmental languages, Meeting deadlines, Navigating through noise, change and overinformation. Making it difficult to align different stakeholders on priorities and the same goal, slowing down progress, and reducing competitive edge.

**Values**

Speed, Focus, Clarity, Inspiration

**Vision**

To create an environment where everyone is in sync, to spend more time moving the needle in the same direction (forward) together, to have an inspired team that believes in the product and the impact they have on the world, to connect with / inspire people to use your product.

**Mission**

Ensure that everyone has the Vision at the front of their mind, at each stage of the product's development. To know not only what to do, but the best / technology way to do it by keeping up to date with and implementing the latest tools. To meet the company/team's full potential.

**Strategy**

All this is enabled through the a dynamic Process, serving as a compass providing direction, a foundation that creates space for innovation / calculated risks, aligns multiple departments by providing clarity on priorities and each individual/teams' contribution to the Vision, and is meticulously defined and known to everyone "like the back of their hand.”

**Tactics**

A streamlined Product Innovation Process, honed over 6 years, is encapsulated in a cloud-based guide. It encompasses the entire product development lifecycle, detailing roles, responsibilities, and stages. The guide serves as a comprehensive resource, outlining product history, purpose, and key development terminologies, while also providing checklists, stage-gate deadlines, and links to essential resources. It recommends automation tools and KPIs aligned with company vision, including templates for key product management documents. It doubles as an effective training tool for product-oriented roles, while evolving over time.

**Benefits Recap**

Builds consensus among stakeholders and investors with deep dive into product progress. It unifies team communication, creating a single source of truth and ensuring quality assurance at every stage. Avoid “FOMO” with updates on the latest tools, like generative AI, reducing administrative tasks and focusing on high-value activities. Teams can adapt quickly, innovate at lower operational costs, and empower members. The process supports seamless transitions for new hires or temporary replacements, identifies inefficiencies, and provides access to best practices in product development without additional hires, countering over-information and optimizing resource allocation.
