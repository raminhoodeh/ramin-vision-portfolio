## Further Projects Info

**nsso (STARTUP):** My start-up and personal flagship product. A personal homepage creator that lets you discover and present all the different sides of yourself — personal and professional — in one beautifully efficient place online. Built with a glass-morphic design aesthetic. Long-term vision includes the NY accelerator and Forbes 30-under-30.

**Dreamsea (APP):** An AI-powered dream journaling and interpretation app (iOS), built with Azin. Draws on Jungian, Freudian, Persian/Zoroastrian, Egyptian, and Japanese dream philosophy to surface meaning from your subconscious. Designed with an ethereal, surrealist visual identity. Live at [nsso.me/dreamsea](http://nsso.me/dreamsea).

**24Seven Concierge (APP):** An AI concierge app — a always-on personal assistant that handles requests, research, and coordination around the clock. Built to demonstrate AI product management capabilities and serve as a real-world portfolio piece.

**Qadam (APP):** A trading and personal intelligence app ([qadam.trade](http://qadam.trade)) that ingests data from multiple APIs — including global tension mapping and tone analysis — to surface actionable insights and trading decisions. Long-term vision includes a quantum computing backend via Q-CTRL or IBM.

**RazinFlix (CREATIVE):** A curated personal film canon and watchlist project shared with Azin — a private streaming curation experience exploring storytelling, cinema, visual language, and creative inspiration. Built because mainstream streaming platforms couldn't capture a truly personal taste.

**MASS SOCIAL WISDOM AGENT (AGENT):** Autonomous AI Agent for Social Wisdom extraction and processing.

**AI-NATIVE PRODUCT OS / PM COURSE (PRODUCTISING):** The process I crafted as a Product Manager, evolved into a full AI-Native Operating System for product work — structured as a 5-Layer Stack (Model → Context → Orchestration → Governance → Human) and an AI Loop (Talk → Decide → Build → Observe → Iterate). Now packaged as a Udemy course: *Transition from Product Manager to AI Product Manager*, planned for release June 2026.

**TEDx (TALK):** My TEDx talk and advocational message to humanity — reminding us of the beauty of our place in the cosmos using mindfulness and a universal perspective, to live a more harmonious and meaningful life.

**THE PROPOSITION (BOOK):** My spiritual fiction novel — a distillation of years of metaphysical enquiry, weaving together philosophy, consciousness, and the nature of reality into an engaging story. Aimed at becoming a bestseller, with long-term ambitions for an anime movie adaptation.

**METACOGNITION:** A way of thinking about thinking — organising stories, pitches, frameworks, and related information into a coherent inner architecture.
