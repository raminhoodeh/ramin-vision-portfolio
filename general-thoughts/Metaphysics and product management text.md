The intersection of product management and metaphysical enquiry:

The quality of a decision can be measured by the degree to which it achieved the goal it set out to do. 

In other words, you have a vision to get to where you want to be, and so you take action to get there. 

And to direct ourselves to where we want to be, we have to first know where we are. 

Therefore, knowledge of where we are is crucial to making good quality decisions. 

Additionally, to fulfil our potential, we must set a vision/goal that represents the maximum possible achievement out of our current and future capabilities. 

As such, knowledge of ourselves; our current and future capabilities, is key to realising our full potential. 

Therefore, to achieve maximum success, we must have knowledge of ourselves, our capabilities, and our place in the world. 

This knowledge of ourselves, our existence and our place within it, is explored in the topic of metaphysics.
