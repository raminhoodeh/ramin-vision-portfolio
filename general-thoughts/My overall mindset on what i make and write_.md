My overall mindset on what i make and write:

It’s all about…

* Making sense of our situation  
* Overcoming over-information and gaining focus  
* Figuring out a vision and working towards it  
* Gaining confidence and clarity

I believe that the quality of a decision can be measured by the degree to which it achieved the goal it set out to do. In other words, you have a vision to get to where you want to be, and so you take action to get there. And to direct ourselves to where we want to be, we have to first know where we are. Therefore, knowledge of where we are is crucial to making good quality decisions. 

Additionally, to fulfil our potential, we must set a vision/goal that represents the maximum possible achievement out of our current and future capabilities. As such, knowledge of ourselves; our current and future capabilities, is key to realising our full potential. Therefore, to achieve maximum success, we must have knowledge of ourselves, our capabilities, and our place in the world. This knowledge of ourselves, our existence and our place within it, is explored in the topic of metaphysics. 

To live a truthful life is to live a life with a clear conscience and a sense of inner peace or confidence. And to do this I would have to first see things for the way they really are, then make the best effort to align my decisions from that truth. 

The theme of my life and work has always been the following: 

**Reduce over-information –** by finding a way to think approach; either all the different sects of metaphysics, overwhelmed with the general buzz of modern life, the business or even the creative world.

**Understanding ourselves–** whether we’re a soul/spirit/psychological self, a business, or a professional/creative. 

**Understanding our place –** in relation to either humanity and existence, a business and the wider market, or ourselves and the job or opportunity market.

**Recognising our beauty/uniqueness –** whether you’re seeing how rare you are as a conscious human, your differentiating factor (resources or skills) as a business, or your unique traits as a professional/creative (

**Recognising the beauty of our purpose** – and therefore wanting to do something about how special we are… being inspired by a vision of where we are in relation to where we could be, as a person undergoing spiritual transformation, or a business, professional or creative person gaining the best opportunities and achieving their full potential.

For example, my TEDx talk is an advocational message to humanity, reminding us of the beauty of our place in existence through mindfulness, explaining how we can use a cosmic perspective to realign our priorities and live a more meaningful life. 

My book, The Proposition, is another distillation of all my metaphysical enquiry, but in an inspiring spiritual fiction story. The book is written in such a way that you can open it on any page and receive wisdom that would remind you how to make more healed and aligned choices. There’s even a “Purpose Guide” in there, too, so you’ve got all your goals and insights in one place. 

My Framework of Reality has dictated my approach to my creative pursuits, my career, a start-up project and even my relationships with others. These have all focused on the same values of gaining clarity and appreciating beauty in all forms. 

This mindset continues in my day job as a Product Lead, documented in my Product Innovation Process, which outlines how multiple teams can overcome all the noise of the business world and align on one goal. This is achieved through a similar existential analysis of the company or team’s place in the market, and its vision, using compelling storytelling to inspire both internal teams and customers on this vision. All your colleagues would rally around this same document, as it’s a single source of truth to have all of the products and process’s information in one place, further helping align everyone in the same direction, together. 

Finally, my start-up project, nsso.me/, is all about gaining clarity on one’s place in the professional world. nsso.me/ is a personal homepage creator that brings together all the different personal and professional parts of you in one place, into a holistic and unified profile. There’s a section for your work experience, social media pages and personal projects or products/services you have to offer. Your nsso.me/ profile can be used as a Resumé as well as a personal shop, or even just a place to help you figure yourself out; to know what your story is. Again, this is all about bringing clarity to find your place in our noisy world, regaining a sense of purpose and recognising your inherent beauty and uniqueness, inspiring yourself and others to do business with you.
